I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Finance and Economics at Warwick Business School.

My research sits at the intersection of financial economics, empirical industrial organization, and structural econometrics. I use structural econometrics to identify and estimate flexible, institutional asset demand systems, which allow me to recover investors' substitution patterns, price elasticities, and alphas. Furthermore, I integrate those demand curves into dynamic corporate finance models to study the feedback between institutional investor demand and firms' financing, investment, and payout policies.
